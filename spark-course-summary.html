July Data Science for Science Residency - Applications Open

PhD students and postdocs across Cambridge have another chance to apply for a funded training course that is successfully equipping scientists with powerful tools to progress their research. The next Data for Science Residency will run for five weeks from Monday 19 July 2021. Applications are open until 21 May 2021. ***What does the course cover?*** The course aims to give researchers in fields outside computer science the skills they need to use machine learning (ML) in their research and helps them apply data analysis to their own datasets and problems. On the course, you will: *use data science techniques on your own datasets and problem; *collaborate on real-world data science challenges in teams as part of two hackathons; and *be part of a world-leading community of scientists and researchers. Residents will cover 6 modules over a period of 5 weeks: *Introduction to Python (preparatory module) *Data Processing with Pandas *Data Visualisation *Web Scraping, JSON and APIs *Introduction to machine learning *Hackathon & presentation
